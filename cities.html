Venice

Venice is a unique city built on a network of canals, and it's famous for its romantic atmosphere, historical significance, and stunning architecture. The city is a UNESCO World Heritage site and is known for landmarks like St. Mark's Square, St. Mark's Basilica, and the Rialto Bridge. Venice is also home to the Gondola rides, offering a picturesque way to explore the waterways.

Milan

Milan is Italy's fashion and design capital, known for its chic shopping districts, modern architecture, and vibrant cultural scene. It's also a hub for business and finance. Key cultural attractions include The Last Supper by Leonardo da Vinci, housed in Santa Maria delle Grazie, and the impressive Milan Cathedral (Duomo di Milano). The city is also famous for its luxury shopping in areas like the Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II.
